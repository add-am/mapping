Description

Abstract / Description:
This dataset outlines areas the covered by documents and maps scheduled under the Environmental Protection (Water and Wetland Biodiversity) Amendment Policy (No.1) 2020. The dataset includes links to online documents and maps.
Purpose:
This dataset outlines areas the covered by documents and maps scheduled under the Environmental Protection (Water and Wetland Biodiversity) Amendment Policy (No.1) 2020. The dataset includes links to online documents and maps. For further information, refer to the following website:
https://environment.des.qld.gov.au/management/water
Additional Information:
Areas scheduled under the Queensland Environmental Protection (Water) Policy (EPP Water) are described in documents and paper / PDF maps. The area these documents and maps applies to are shown in this dataset, along with links to the online documents and maps themselves.
