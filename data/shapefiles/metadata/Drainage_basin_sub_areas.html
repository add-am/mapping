Description

Abstract / Description:
This dataset contains the extent, name and unique ID number of the Basin Sub Areas (subdivisions of major drainage basins) in the State of Queensland.
Purpose:
To provide an authorative single point of truth for the extent of Basin Sub Areas in the State Queensland. It is suitable for use in land administration, emergency and water management, monitoring of climate change, hydrological modelling, topographic and vegetation mapping and in the production of navigational and web based mapping applications.
Additional Information:
The dataset's original purpose was to implant some location identifier into the numbering system of bores and gauging stations. For example all bore numbers were prefixed with a four-digit number. The first three were used to locate the bore into a specific major river basin .The forth digit was used to locate the bore into a subdivision of that major river basin as shown in the Basin Sub Areas - Queensland coverage. This coverage is now only used as a means of numbering bores and gauging stations.

The dataset was captured at 1:100 000.

The seaward edge of this data aligns with the Coastline and State Border - Queensland dataset.

History:

Lineage statement:
This data set was created as a consequence of a larger project involving the capturing of all gauging station catchments in Queensland.
The polygons were constructed by:
- Using the existing major & minor river basins as an external framework
- Creating a clear overlay matching every 1:100 000 topographical map in Queensland
- The clear overlay had all 1:250 000 river basin boundaries, the major and minor river basins as well as the corner points for each map sheet plotted on it
- Using these 1:250 000 basin boundaries, the major and minor river basins and the topography from the 1: 100 000 topo maps as a guide, the Sub-Basin Areas were determined and traced on to the clear overlay
- These basins were then digitised and stored in an ArcInfo coverage
- RMS errors not > than 0.005
- Regions were created from the resulting polygons
- Attributes pertaining to these regions are: Sub_Name; Sub_Number as definded by the Australian Water Resources Management Committee (WRMC).

River Basins are subsets of and have boundaries that are aligned to and nested with the associated Drainage Basin.
Please refer to the Drainage Basin - Queensland dataset.

In 2020, the seaward edge of the data was moved to align with the Coastline and State Border - Queensland dataset.
