Description

Abstract / Description:
This dataset outlines the management intent for the waters scheduled under the Environmental Protection (Water and Wetland Biodiversity) Policy 2019. Please refer to the following website for further information:
https://environment.des.qld.gov.au/management/water/policy
Purpose:
The MI_Type field provides the level of protection required within the area represented by a polygon.

The MI_Type field has the following attributes:
HEV - High Environmental Value
SD - Slightly Disturbed
SD islands - Refers to coastal islands and is used in exactly the same way as SD data.
MD - Moderately Disturbed
HD - Highly Disturbed

Fitzroy and SEQ have additional attributes that are slightly different. These will be phased out as updates are made.

They include:

HEVm_fw (HEV maintain freshwater) - Treat polygons as HEV
HEVm_mar (HEV maintain marine) - Treat polygons as HEV

HEVa_fw (HEV achieve freshwater)- Treat these polygons as SD
HEVa_mar (HEV achieve marine) - Treat these polygons as SD

ENV_VALUE_ZONE: This field relates to areas identified in the scheduled document, available on the following website:
https://environment.des.qld.gov.au/management/water/policy

Please note the recommended viewing scale: 1:25000
Additional Information:
Regarding the Gregory River Estuary:
In areas where the Gregory River estuary has not been mapped, please refer to the applicable mid-estuary aquatic ecosystem water quality objectives as provided in the Proserpine schedule document.
